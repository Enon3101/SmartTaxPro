Business Services FAQ

Which business structure is best for me?

+

The best business structure depends on factors like liability protection, tax benefits, funding requirements, and compliance burden. We can help you choose the most suitable structure based on your business needs and goals.

How long does company registration take?

+

Company registration typically takes 15-20 working days for Private Limited Companies and LLPs, 7-10 days for Partnership Firms, and 2-3 days for Proprietorship registration.

What are the ongoing compliance requirements?

+

Ongoing compliance includes annual ROC filings, GST returns, income tax returns, TDS compliance, and other regulatory requirements depending on your business type and size.

Do I need a business bank account?

+

Yes, it's mandatory for companies and LLPs to have a separate business bank account. For proprietorship, while not mandatory, it's highly recommended for better financial management.

What documents are required for registration?

+

Required documents include PAN, Aadhaar, address proof, business plan, bank statements, and other documents depending on the business structure. We provide a complete checklist during the process.
