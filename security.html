Security FAQ

Is my tax data safe with MyeCA.in?

Yes, absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and security measures to protect your data. Your information is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Do you share my data with third parties?

No, we never share your personal or financial data with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

How do you protect against data breaches?

We use multiple layers of security including encryption, access controls, regular security audits, and 24/7 monitoring to prevent breaches.

Can I delete my data from your servers?

Yes, you can request complete deletion of your data at any time. We'll remove all your information permanently within 30 days.

What happens if there's a security incident?

We have a comprehensive incident response plan. In case of any security incident, we'll notify affected users immediately and take appropriate action.

How often do you conduct security audits?

We conduct comprehensive security audits annually, with additional penetration testing and vulnerability assessments throughout the year.
