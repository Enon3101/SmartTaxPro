üìä TAX CALCULATION FORMULAE
===========================

1. INCOME TAX CALCULATIONS
--------------------------

OLD TAX REGIME (FY 2023-24):
----------------------------
Up to ‚Çπ2.5 lakhs: Nil
‚Çπ2.5 lakhs to ‚Çπ5 lakhs: 5% of (Total Income - ‚Çπ2.5 lakhs)
‚Çπ5 lakhs to ‚Çπ10 lakhs: ‚Çπ12,500 + 20% of (Total Income - ‚Çπ5 lakhs)
Above ‚Çπ10 lakhs: ‚Çπ1,12,500 + 30% of (Total Income - ‚Çπ10 lakhs)

NEW TAX REGIME (FY 2023-24):
----------------------------
Up to ‚Çπ3 lakhs: Nil
‚Çπ3 lakhs to ‚Çπ6 lakhs: 5% of (Total Income - ‚Çπ3 lakhs)
‚Çπ6 lakhs to ‚Çπ9 lakhs: ‚Çπ15,000 + 10% of (Total Income - ‚Çπ6 lakhs)
‚Çπ9 lakhs to ‚Çπ12 lakhs: ‚Çπ45,000 + 15% of (Total Income - ‚Çπ9 lakhs)
‚Çπ12 lakhs to ‚Çπ15 lakhs: ‚Çπ90,000 + 20% of (Total Income - ‚Çπ12 lakhs)
Above ‚Çπ15 lakhs: ‚Çπ1,50,000 + 30% of (Total Income - ‚Çπ15 lakhs)

2. HRA EXEMPTION CALCULATION
----------------------------
HRA Exemption = Minimum of:
1. Actual HRA received
2. 50% of basic salary (metro cities) / 40% (non-metro)
3. Rent paid - 10% of basic salary

Formula: min(Actual HRA, 50% of Basic, Rent - 10% of Basic)

3. TDS CALCULATIONS
-------------------
TDS on Salary (Section 192):
- Calculate total taxable income
- Apply applicable tax slab
- Divide by 12 for monthly TDS

TDS on Interest (Section 194A):
- 10% on interest income above ‚Çπ40,000 (‚Çπ50,000 for senior citizens)

TDS on Rent (Section 194I):
- 2% on rent for machinery/equipment
- 10% on rent for land/building

4. CAPITAL GAINS CALCULATIONS
-----------------------------
Short Term Capital Gains (STCG):
- Holding period < 12 months (equity) / < 36 months (others)
- Tax rate: 15% (equity) / Normal slab rate (others)

Long Term Capital Gains (LTCG):
- Holding period ‚â• 12 months (equity) / ‚â• 36 months (others)
- Tax rate: 10% above ‚Çπ1 lakh (equity) / 20% with indexation (others)

Formula: Sale Price - Purchase Price - Indexation Benefit

5. TAKE-HOME SALARY CALCULATION
-------------------------------
Basic Salary: Base amount
HRA: House Rent Allowance
DA: Dearness Allowance
Special Allowance: Other allowances
Gross Salary = Basic + HRA + DA + Special Allowance

Deductions:
- PF: 12% of Basic
- Professional Tax: As per state
- TDS: As calculated

Net Salary = Gross Salary - Total Deductions

6. INVESTMENT CALCULATORS
-------------------------

SIP CALCULATOR:
---------------
Future Value = P √ó [(1 + r)^n - 1] √ó (1 + r) / r
Where:
P = Monthly investment
r = Monthly interest rate (annual rate / 12)
n = Number of months

FD CALCULATOR:
--------------
Maturity Amount = Principal √ó (1 + r)^n
Where:
r = Annual interest rate
n = Number of years

PPF CALCULATOR:
---------------
Maturity Amount = P √ó [(1 + r)^n - 1] / r
Where:
P = Annual contribution
r = Interest rate (7.1% for FY 2023-24)
n = Number of years

7. LOAN EMI CALCULATIONS
------------------------
EMI = P √ó r √ó (1 + r)^n / [(1 + r)^n - 1]
Where:
P = Principal amount
r = Monthly interest rate (annual rate / 12 / 100)
n = Total number of months

Total Interest = (EMI √ó n) - P
Total Amount = EMI √ó n

8. GST CALCULATIONS
-------------------
GST Amount = Base Amount √ó GST Rate / 100
Total Amount = Base Amount + GST Amount

GST Rates:
- 0%: Essential items
- 5%: Basic necessities
- 12%: Standard items
- 18%: Most goods and services
- 28%: Luxury items
