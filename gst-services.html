GST Frequently Asked Questions

Who needs GST registration?

+

GST registration is mandatory for businesses with annual turnover above ‚Çπ20 lakhs (‚Çπ10 lakhs for special category states). It's also required for inter-state supplies, e-commerce operators, and certain specific business activities.

What is the composition scheme?

+

The composition scheme is for small businesses with turnover up to ‚Çπ1.5 crores. It offers simplified compliance with lower tax rates and quarterly filing instead of monthly returns.

What are the GST return filing deadlines?

+

GSTR-1 is due by 11th of next month, GSTR-3B by 20th of next month, GSTR-9 (annual) by 31st December, and GSTR-9C (audit) by 31st December of the following year.

How to claim input tax credit?

+

Input tax credit can be claimed on purchases used for business purposes. The supplier must have filed GSTR-1 and you must have received the goods/services. ITC is claimed in GSTR-3B.

What are the penalties for late filing?

+

Late filing attracts a penalty of ‚Çπ50 per day (‚Çπ20 for nil returns) and interest at 18% per annum on the tax amount. Additional penalties may apply for continued non-compliance.
