Annual Tax Timeline

April

Financial Year Begins

New financial year starts. Begin collecting documents and tracking income.

  • Start maintaining records
  • Track all income sources
  • Plan tax-saving investments
June

First Advance Tax Payment

Pay first installment of advance tax if your tax liability exceeds ₹10,000.

  • Calculate advance tax liability
  • Pay 15% of total tax
  • Keep payment receipts
September

Second Advance Tax Payment

Pay second installment of advance tax (45% of total tax).

  • Review income till date
  • Calculate revised liability
  • Pay additional amount if needed
December

Third Advance Tax Payment

Pay third installment of advance tax (75% of total tax).

  • Assess annual income
  • Plan final payment
  • Review tax-saving options
March

Financial Year Ends

Complete all tax-saving investments and finalize documents.

  • Make last-minute investments
  • Collect all documents
  • Prepare for filing
May

Form 16 Collection

Employers must issue Form 16 to employees by May 31st.

  • Request Form 16 from employer
  • Verify details in Form 16
  • Collect other certificates
July

Income Tax Return Filing

Last date for filing ITR for individuals (July 31st).

  • File ITR before deadline
  • Pay any remaining tax
  • Keep acknowledgment receipt
October

Business Tax Filing

Last date for filing ITR for businesses and audit cases.

  • Complete audit if required
  • File business ITR
  • Pay any additional tax
