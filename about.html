About MyeCA.in

Simplifying tax filing for millions of Indians since 2020

Our Story

MyeCA.in was born from a simple observation: tax filing in India was unnecessarily complex and intimidating for the average person. Founded in 2020 by a team of tax professionals and technology experts, we set out to democratize tax filing and make it accessible to everyone.

What started as a small startup has grown into one of India's most trusted tax filing platforms, serving over 2 million users across the country. Our mission remains the same: to make tax filing simple, secure, and stress-free.
