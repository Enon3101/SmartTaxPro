Investment Services FAQ

How do I start investing in mutual funds?

+

To start investing in mutual funds, you need to complete KYC, choose the right funds based on your goals and risk profile, and start with SIP or lump sum investment. Our advisors can help you select the best funds.

What is the minimum amount for SIP?

+

Most mutual funds allow SIP investments starting from ‚Çπ500 per month. Some funds may have higher minimum amounts. You can start with a small amount and increase it gradually.

How much life insurance do I need?

+

Life insurance coverage should be 10-15 times your annual income. Consider factors like dependents, loans, and future expenses. Our insurance calculator can help determine the right coverage amount.

Which tax-saving investments are best?

+

ELSS funds offer the best combination of tax benefits and growth potential. Other options include PPF, NPS, and tax-saving FDs. The choice depends on your risk appetite and investment horizon.

How to plan for retirement?

+

Start early, calculate your retirement corpus, choose the right retirement products like NPS, PPF, or pension plans, and maintain a diversified portfolio. Our retirement calculator can help plan your corpus.
