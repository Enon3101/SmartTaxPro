Tax Filing Questions

Common questions about income tax filing process and requirements

You'll need Form 16 from your employer, bank statements, investment proofs (ELSS, PPF, etc.), rent receipts, medical bills, and any other income documents. Our platform will guide you through the exact documents needed based on your income sources.

With MyeCA.in, most users complete their tax filing in 15-30 minutes. The process includes document upload, automatic calculation, review, and e-filing. Our AI-powered system pre-fills most information to save time.

If you miss the deadline, you can still file a belated return. However, you may face penalties and interest charges. We recommend filing before the deadline to avoid these charges. Our platform sends timely reminders to help you stay compliant.

You can track your refund through our platform's dashboard, the Income Tax Department website, or the TDS CPC website. We also send notifications when your refund is processed. Typically, refunds are processed within 2-4 weeks of filing.

GST Services Questions

Questions about GST registration, filing, and compliance

GST registration is mandatory for businesses with annual turnover exceeding ₹20 lakhs (₹10 lakhs for special category states). E-commerce sellers, interstate suppliers, and businesses making taxable supplies must also register regardless of turnover.

Regular taxpayers file GSTR-1 (monthly/quarterly), GSTR-3B (monthly), and GSTR-9 (annually). Composition dealers file GSTR-4 quarterly. Our platform sends timely reminders and helps you file all returns on time.

Late filing attracts a penalty of ₹50 per day (₹20 for nil returns) and interest at 18% per annum on the tax amount. Our platform helps you avoid these penalties with automated reminders and easy filing process.

Yes, you can claim input tax credit on GST paid on purchases used for business purposes. Our platform automatically calculates and claims eligible ITC, ensuring you don't miss any credits and maximize your tax savings.
