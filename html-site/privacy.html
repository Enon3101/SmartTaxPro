1. Introduction

At MyeCA.in, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our tax filing services.

Your Privacy Matters: We understand the sensitive nature of tax and financial information, and we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines our commitment to protecting your data.

Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of MyeCA.in, including visitors to our website, registered users, and customers who use our tax filing services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Date of birth and gender
  • PAN (Permanent Account Number) and Aadhaar number
  • Address and contact details
  • Bank account information for refunds

Tax and Financial Information

  • Income details and sources
  • Investment and deduction information
  • Tax documents and certificates
  • Previous tax filing history
  • Financial statements and receipts

Technical Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Usage patterns and preferences
  • Cookies and tracking data

Usage Information

  • Pages visited and features used
  • Time spent on different sections
  • Search queries and interactions
  • Error logs and performance data

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Payment processing and billing
  • Cloud storage and hosting
  • Customer support and communication
  • Analytics and performance monitoring

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, including:

  • Compliance with tax regulations
  • Response to legal requests or court orders
  • Protection of our rights and property
  • Prevention of fraud or security threats

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

Government Authorities: We may be required to share information with government authorities for tax compliance and regulatory purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure socket layer (SSL) technology
  • Firewall and intrusion detection systems
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access

Organizational Measures

  • Employee training on data protection
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Regular security assessments
  • Incident response procedures

Physical Security

  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Environmental controls and backup systems
  • Restricted access to server facilities

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

Retention Periods

  • Account Information: Until account deletion or 7 years after last activity
  • Tax Returns: 7 years (as required by tax law)
  • Financial Documents: 7 years after filing
  • Payment Information: 7 years for accounting purposes
  • Usage Data: 2 years for analytics and improvement

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal requirements for data retention. We will securely delete your data within 30 days of your request.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal information
  • Receive your data in a portable format
  • Know how your information is being used

Correction and Updates

  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Complete incomplete information
  • Modify your preferences and settings

Deletion and Restriction

  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Restrict processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Objection and Complaints

  • Object to processing of your information
  • Lodge complaints with supervisory authorities
  • Seek legal remedies for privacy violations

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

Types of Cookies

  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand usage patterns

Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our services.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

11. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than India. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards.

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending email notifications to registered users
  • Displaying prominent notices on our platform

Your continued use of our services after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
