Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common tax questions

When is the tax filing deadline? +
For AY 2025-26, the ITR filing deadline is July 31, 2025 for non-audit cases and October 31, 2025 for audit cases. If you need more time, you can request an extension, but any taxes owed are still due by the original deadline.
How long does it take to receive my tax refund? +
Most e-filed returns with direct deposit are processed within 21 days. Paper returns typically take 6-8 weeks. You can check your refund status on the Income Tax Department website using your PAN and Assessment Year.
What documents do I need to file my taxes? +
Common documents include: Form 16 from employers, Form 26AS, AIS, PAN card, bank statements, investment proofs, rent receipts, and last year's tax return. Additional documents may be needed depending on your specific situation.

What if I make a mistake on my tax return? +
If you discover an error after filing, you can file a revised return using the same ITR form. Depending on the mistake, the Income Tax Department may correct simple errors automatically or contact you if more information is needed.
