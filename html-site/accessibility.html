Our Commitment to Accessibility

At MyeCA.in, we believe that tax filing should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their abilities or disabilities. We are committed to ensuring that our website and services are usable by people with diverse needs and abilities.

Accessibility Goal: We strive to provide an inclusive experience that allows all users to file their taxes independently and with confidence.

Assistive Technology Support

Our website is compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen Readers: JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver, TalkBack
  • Screen Magnifiers: ZoomText, Windows Magnifier
  • Voice Recognition: Dragon NaturallySpeaking, Voice Control
  • Switch Navigation: Switch devices and alternative input methods
  • Braille Displays: Refreshable braille devices

Testing and Evaluation

We regularly test our website for accessibility using multiple methods:

  • Automated Testing: Regular scans using accessibility testing tools
  • Manual Testing: Keyboard navigation and screen reader testing
  • User Testing: Feedback from users with disabilities
  • Expert Review: Accessibility audits by certified professionals
  • Continuous Monitoring: Ongoing accessibility improvements

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, we acknowledge the following limitations:

  • Some third-party calculators may have limited accessibility features
  • Complex tax forms may require additional time for screen reader users
  • Certain interactive elements may need alternative access methods
Our Commitment: We are continuously working to address these limitations and improve accessibility across all features.
