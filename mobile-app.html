Frequently Asked Questions

Is the mobile app free to download?

Yes, the MyeCA.in mobile app is completely free to download. You only pay for the tax filing service when you use it.

Can I use the app without internet?

Basic features like viewing saved documents and using calculators work offline. Internet is required for filing and syncing data.

Is my data synced between mobile and desktop?

Yes, all your data is automatically synced across all devices. Start on mobile, continue on desktop - seamless experience.

Which devices are supported?

iOS: iPhone 6s and later, iPad (iOS 13.0+). Android: All devices running Android 8.0 or later.

Can I file taxes for my family members?

Yes, you can manage multiple tax filings for family members from a single account with proper authorization.

How secure is the mobile app?

The app uses the same bank-level security as our website, including biometric authentication and end-to-end encryption.
